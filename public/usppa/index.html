The Association

What is the USPPA?

The United States Polo Patrons Association (USPPA) is a patron-first framework for building and sustaining polo as a high-trust, transparent ecosystem.

Instead of hiding patronage behind private checks and closed doors, USPPA makes the entire patronage flow transparent and programmable. Chapters, arenas, horses, and players can all be recognized and rewarded from the same shared ledger.

USPPA is also the governing umbrella for the Cowboy Polo Circuit — a national development league where riders can progress with as little as one good horse, and patrons can see exactly what their support is building.

Polo Patronium

The Patronage Utility Token

Polo Patronium (PATRON) is the on-chain utility token used by USPPA to measure and distribute patronage across horses, riders, and chapters. It is not equity in any horse or team; instead, it is the accounting tool for who has shown up to support the game.

  • Patrons add USDC on Base through the Patron Wallet (hosted on the Cowboy Polo MVP).
  • Confirmed patronage mints or credits PATRON to the same wallet on a 1:1 basis with USD contributed (subject to program rules).
  • Separate reward modules can allocate additional PATRON to founding patrons, stewards, or specific campaigns.

Your Patron Wallet — created with a simple email sign-up — works across:

  • Cowboy Polo Circuit
  • USPPA Chapters & Arenas
  • Future Patronium-enabled programs

Horses Raised in Public

The Three Sevens 7̶7̶7̶ Remuda is the managed string of USPPA horses that live inside the Cowboy Polo Circuit. Every sanctioned chukker, every rider combination, and every chapter that touches a horse adds to its story.

Instead of a single sale moment, patrons see:

  • Saddle time from first rides to retirement
  • Performance snapshots across multiple seasons
  • How horses, riders, and arenas are interlinked over time

Cowboy Polo is the testing ground; USPPA is the ledger that keeps score for the whole network.

Roadmap

How USPPA Rolls Out

  1. Cowboy Polo MVP Launch: Patron Wallet + basic PATRON flows live on the Cowboy Polo site.
  2. Chapter Onboarding: Early arenas and programs come online as Cowboy Polo chapters with simple chukker reporting.
  3. Patronium Rewards: Configurable reward modules distribute PATRON for founding patrons, stewards, and specific horses.
  4. Full USPPA Registry: Public listings for chapters, horses, riders, and patronage programs.
  5. Streaming Integration: Cowboy Polo and future field polo streamed via The Polo Way, with Patronium used to recognize viewership and support.

All of this begins with one working product: the Cowboy Polo MVP and Patron Wallet. That is where you can sign up, fund patronage, and receive PATRON today.
